I'm a Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering MS student at **New York University** (GPA 3.7),
working as a Research Assistant at the
[Generalizable Robotics & AI Lab (CILVR)](https://cilvr.nyu.edu).
 
My work focuses on **autonomous navigation**, **visual SLAM**, and **embedded perception** —
building real robot systems that work in the real world, not just in simulation.
I'm particularly excited about making capable robots accessible and affordable,
as reflected in my published work on the YOR mobile manipulator platform.
 
Previously, I was a Graduate Apprentice at **Bosch Chassis Systems**, where I led
23 digitization projects and won 1st place in Bosch's MDP Global Championship.
 
I'm actively looking for **full-time roles** in robotics software engineering at
mission-driven startups and mid-size companies. If you're building something
interesting with robots, [let's talk](mailto:nehanavarkar@nyu.edu).
